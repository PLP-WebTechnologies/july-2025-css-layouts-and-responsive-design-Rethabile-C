Introduction

Mental health is an important part of our lives. We have to take a good care of our mental health to achieve a holistic wellbeing.

Here is a list of activities that improve mental wellbeing:

  • Meditation

    • Meditation relaxes the mind and reduce overthinking. Meditation can be done in the morning or evening.

  • Exercise

    • Exercising releases endorphins which are happy hormones. It also improves physical health.

  • Eating healthy food

    • Eating healthy food leads to improved physical and mental wellbeing.

About US

We are a non-profit organization that aims to create awareness about mental health in Africa.

We believe that mental health is as important as physical health and we strive to provide resources and support to those in need.

This organisation started in 2025 founded by a group of students who had mental health challenges, they believed that by supporting eachother they can overcome mental health problems.
